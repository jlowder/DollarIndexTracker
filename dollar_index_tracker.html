What is the Dollar Index?

The U.S. Dollar Index (DXY) is a measure of the value of the United States dollar relative to a basket of foreign currencies. It was established in 1973 and is maintained by ICE (Intercontinental Exchange).

Currency Basket:

  • Euro (EUR): 57.6%
  • Japanese Yen (JPY): 13.6%
  • British Pound (GBP): 11.9%
  • Canadian Dollar (CAD): 9.1%
  • Swedish Krona (SEK): 4.2%
  • Swiss Franc (CHF): 3.6%

  • Range Selector: Use the buttons above the chart for quick date ranges
